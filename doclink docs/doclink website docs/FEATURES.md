# DocLink Features Documentation

This document provides comprehensive information about all features available in the DocLink platform.

## Overview

DocLink is a comprehensive healthcare platform offering multiple services to connect patients with healthcare providers in Pakistan. The platform is designed to make healthcare accessible, affordable, and available 24/7.

## Core Features

### 1. Subscription Plans

**Description**: Affordable subscription-based healthcare access that keeps you connected with your own doctor 24/7.

**Key Benefits:**
- **24/7 Doctor Access**: Stay connected with your own doctor anytime, anywhere
- **Affordable Pricing**: Starting at PKR 699 with special discounts (up to 50% off)
- **Continuous Care**: Maintain continuity of care with the same doctor
- **Multiple Consultation Modes**: Chat, audio, or video consultations

**How It Works:**
1. Choose a subscription plan
2. Get assigned to a doctor or select your preferred doctor
3. Access healthcare services anytime through the app
4. Unlimited consultations within subscription period

**Available Plans:**
- Monthly subscriptions
- Quarterly subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions
- Family plans (coming soon)

**Page Location**: `/subscription-plans`

---

### 2. Lab Tests

- **Integration with Medical Records**: Test results automatically saved to your profile

**Popular Tests:**
