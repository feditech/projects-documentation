## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Web Application:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Stable internet connection
- Recommended screen resolution: 1024x768 or higher

**Mobile App:**
- Android 6.0 or higher
- iOS 12 or higher (coming soon)
- Minimum 100 MB free storage

### Accessing DocLink

1. **Website**: Visit [doclink.com](https://doclink.com) or your configured URL
2. **Mobile App**: Download from Google Play Store
3. **Direct Link**: Use the link sent via SMS/email

## Creating an Account

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Visit DocLink Website**
   - Go to the DocLink homepage
   - Click on "Sign Up" or "Register"

2. **Enter Basic Information**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)

3. **Verify Your Account**
   - Enter OTP sent to your phone
   - Verify email address via link

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Date of Birth
   - Gender
   - Address
   - Emergency Contact

5. **Choose Your Plan**
   - Browse available subscription plans
   - Select the plan that suits you
   - Complete payment

### Account Security

- Use a strong password
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Never share your login credentials
- Log out from shared devices

## Subscription Plans

### Available Plans

- **Price**: PKR 699 (50% off promotional price)

- **Features**:
  - Unlimited consultations
  - 24/7 doctor access
  - Digital prescriptions
  - Medical records storage

#### Quarterly Plan
- **Price**: PKR 1,899 (save 10%)
- **Duration**: 90 days
- **All Monthly Plan features included**

- **Price**: PKR 6,999 (save 20%)
- **Duration**: 365 days
- **All Monthly Plan features included**

3. **Check Availability**: Ensure doctor matches your schedule

If your existing doctor isn't on DocLink:

1. **Visit "Invite Your Doctor" page**

4. **Track Status**: We'll notify you when your doctor joins

## Consultations

### Types of Consultations

1. **Video Consultation**
   - Face-to-face interaction
   - Most recommended for detailed assessment
   - Requires good internet connection

2. **Audio Consultation**
   - Voice call with doctor
   - Good for follow-ups
   - Works with slower internet

3. **Chat Consultation**
   - Text-based messaging
   - Good for quick questions
   - Can include photos

### Booking a Consultation

1. **Choose Consultation Type**
2. **Select Doctor** (or use your assigned doctor)
3. **Select Date and Time**
4. **Describe Your Symptoms**
5. **Confirm Booking**

### During Consultation

**Preparation:**
- Have your medical history ready
- List your current medications
- Note down your symptoms
- Prepare questions for doctor

**During Call:**
- Be in a quiet, well-lit place
- Have stable internet connection
- Be honest about symptoms
- Take notes during consultation

**After Consultation:**
- Review digital prescription
- Follow doctor's instructions
- Schedule follow-up if needed
- Save consultation notes

### Getting Your Prescription

- Prescriptions sent digitally
- Available in your account
- Can be downloaded/printed
- Shareable with pharmacies

## Medical Records

### Uploading Records

1. **Access Medical Records Section**
2. **Click "Upload New Record"**
3. **Select Record Type**:
   - Prescription
   - Lab Report
   - Imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT scan)
   - Vaccination Record
   - Hospital Discharge Summary
4. **Upload File** (PDF, JPG, PNG)
5. **Add Details**:
   - Date of record
   - Doctor/Hospital name
   - Notes/Description
6. **Save Record**

### Organizing Records

- **Folders**: Organize by type or date
- **Tags**: Add tags for easy searching
- **Chronological View**: See records timeline
- **Search**: Find specific records quickly

### Sharing Records

**With Doctors:**
- Select records to share
- Choose doctor
- Grant access
- Doctor can view during consultation

**With Family:**
- Add family member to account
- Grant access to specific records
- Revoke access anytime

### Privacy & Security

- All records encrypted
- Only you control access
- Secure cloud backup
- Industry-standard compliant storage

## FAQs

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime. See our cancellation policy for details.

**Q: Is auto-renewal enabled by default?**
A: Yes, but you can disable it in settings.

**Q: Can I change my plan?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime.

### Consultation Questions

**Q: How quickly can I consult a doctor?**
A: With an active subscription, usually within minutes during business hours.

**Q: Can I choose my doctor?**
A: Yes, you can select your preferred doctor or get assigned one.

**Q: Are consultations recorded?**
A: Video consultations are not recorded. Notes and prescriptions are saved.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What if I have connection issues during consultation?**
A: Switch to audio call or chat. Contact support if problems persist.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and follow healthcare data protection standards.
