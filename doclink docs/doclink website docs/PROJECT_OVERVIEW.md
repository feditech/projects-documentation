# DocLink - Project Overview

## Introduction

DocLink is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform designed to revolutionize primary healthcare delivery in Pakistan. The platform connects patients with their own doctors through a subscription-based model, making healthcare affordable, accessible, and available 24/7.

## Mission

To connect the entire population of Pakistan with their own Doctors, providing seamless healthcare access at minimum cost.

## Vision

To build a subscription-based digital healthcare system in Pakistan, making healthcare affordable and accessible to all at minimum cost.

## What is DocLink?

DocLink is a web application that bridges the gap between patients and healthcare providers by offering:

- **24/7 Doctor Connectivity**: Stay connected with your own doctor anytime, anywhere
- **Subscription-Based Model**: Affordable healthcare through subscription plans
- **Comprehensive Healthcare Services**: From consultations to lab tests and medical records
- **Digital First**: Minimize clinic visits while maintaining quality healthcare

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Patients**: Individuals seeking convenient, affordable healthcare access
- **Healthcare Providers**: Doctors looking to expand their digital presence and reach more patients

### Geographic Focus
- Primary market: Pakistan
- Focused on making healthcare accessible across urban and rural areas

## Key Differentiators

1. **Subscription Model**: Unlike pay-per-visit models, DocLink offers affordable subscription plans
2. **Own Doctor Connection**: Patients can maintain continuity of care with their own doctor
3. **Comprehensive Platform**: Integration of consultations, lab tests, and medical records
4. **Accessibility**: 24/7 availability with minimal infrastructure requirements
5. **Affordability**: Designed specifically for the Pakistani market with competitive pricing

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend Framework**: Next.js 12.3.1 (React 17)
- **UI Components**: Material-UI (MUI) v5
- **State Management**: Redux with Redux Thunk
- **Styling**: Styled Components + Emotion + Sass
- **HTTP Client**: Axios
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Media**: React Player
- **Date Handling**: Moment.js and date-fns

## Project Status

Active development - The platform is currently operational and serving users in Pakistan.
