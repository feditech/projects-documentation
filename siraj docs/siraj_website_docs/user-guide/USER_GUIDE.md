## MBTI Personality Test

### About the Test

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a widely-used personality assessment that identifies your:
- **Energy source:** Extroversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)
- **Information processing:** Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)
- **Decision-making:** Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)
- **Lifestyle approach:** Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)

### Taking the Test

#### Before You Start
- Find a quiet space without distractions
- Allow 15-30 minutes to complete
- Answer honestly based on your natural preferences
- There are no "right" or "wrong" answers
- Think about how you usually behave, not how you'd like to be

#### During the Test
1. Read each question carefully
2. Select the option that best describes you
3. Use the "Next" and "Back" buttons to navigate
4. Your progress is automatically saved
5. You can pause and resume later

#### Test Structure
- **60-80 questions** covering different personality aspects
- Questions are presented one at a time
- Multiple-choice format
- Progress indicator shows completion status

#### Submitting the Test
1. Review your answers if needed
2. Click "Submit" on the final question
3. Wait for results processing (usually 1-2 minutes)

### Test Retakes

**Retake Policy:**
- You can request a test retake if you feel your results don't reflect your true personality
- Submit a retake request from your profile page
- The Siraj team will review and approve legitimate requests
- Approved retakes are notified via your account

**When to consider a retake:**
- You rushed through the questions
- You were distracted during the test
- Your life circumstances have significantly changed
- You didn't understand some questions

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem: Not receiving OTP code**
- **Solution:**
  - Check WhatsApp is working properly
  - Verify phone number is correct
  - Wait full 2 minutes before requesting new code
  - Check spam/archived messages
  - Try a different browser

**Problem: OTP code expired**
- **Solution:**
  - Request a new code
  - Enter code within 5 minutes

**Problem: Too many login attempts**
- **Solution:**
  - Wait 2 minutes
  - Try again with correct phone number

### Test Issues

**Problem: Test not loading**
- **Solution:**
  - Refresh the page
  - Clear browser cache
  - Try a different browser
  - Check internet connection

**Problem: Can't submit test**
- **Solution:**
  - Ensure all questions are answered
  - Check internet connection
  - Try submitting again
  - Contact support if persists

**Problem: Lost test progress**
- **Solution:**
  - Progress usually auto-saves
  - Log back in to continue
  - If truly lost, contact support

### Booking Issues

**Problem: Can't book counselor (Profile not complete)**
- **Solution:**
  - Complete your profile in Settings
  - Add all required information
  - Try booking again

**Problem: Can't book counselor (Test required)**
- **Solution:**
  - Complete MBTI personality test first
  - Wait for results
  - Then proceed with booking

**Problem: No available time slots**
- **Solution:**
  - Try different advisors
  - Check back later for new slots
  - Book in advance for popular times

### Technical Issues

**Problem: Page not loading**
- **Solution:**
  - Refresh the page
  - Clear browser cache and cookies
  - Try incognito/private mode
  - Update your browser
  - Try different browser

**Problem: Language issues**
- **Solution:**
  - Use language switcher (top right)
  - Select preferred language
  - Page will reload in chosen language

**Problem: Mobile display issues**
- **Solution:**
  - Rotate device to landscape
  - Update your browser
  - Clear app cache
  - Use latest mobile browser version

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About the Test

**Q: How long does the MBTI test take?**  
A: Typically 15-30 minutes, depending on how much time you take to consider each question.

**Q: Can I pause the test and come back later?**  
A: Yes, your progress is automatically saved. Just log back in to continue.

**Q: How accurate is the test?**  
A: The MBTI is a widely-used and validated personality assessment. However, results are most accurate when you answer honestly and thoughtfully.

**Q: Can my personality type change?**  
A: Your core personality tends to be stable, but life experiences can influence how you express it. Retakes may show slight variations.

### About Counseling

**Q: Do I need to take the test before booking?**  
A: Yes, counselors use your results to provide personalized guidance.

**Q: How long is a counseling session?**  
A: Typically 45-60 minutes.

**Q: Can I choose my advisor?**  
A: Yes, you can browse advisor profiles and select based on your preferences.

**Q: What if I need to reschedule?**  
A: Contact support at least 24 hours before your session to reschedule.

### About Privacy

**Q: Is my information secure?**  
A: Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

**Q: Who can see my results?**  
A: Only you and counselors you book sessions with can see your detailed results.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes, you can permanently delete your account anytime from Settings.

### About Billing

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept major credit cards and regional payment methods.

**Q: Are there refunds?**  
A: Refund policies vary by plan. Contact support for specific cases.

**Q: Do sessions expire?**  
A: Yes, sessions typically expire based on your plan. Check your plan details.
