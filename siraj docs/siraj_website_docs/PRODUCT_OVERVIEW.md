# Siraj - Product Overview

## ğŸ¯ What is Siraj?

**Siraj** (meaning "lamp" or "light" in Arabic) is a comprehensive career guidance platform designed to empower students across the Arab world in making informed academic and career decisions. Through personalized assessments, expert counseling, hands-on trial programs, and a comprehensive college exploration hub, Siraj helps students discover their strengths and align them with the right career path.

### Mission Statement

To guide students in making informed decisions and achieving their full potential by providing personalized career guidance, comprehensive resources, and expert support throughout their academic journey.

## ğŸŒŸ Key Features

### 1. MBTI Personality Assessment
- **16 personality types** based on Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)
- Comprehensive personality traits analysis
- Identification of natural strengths and preferences
- Career path recommendations tailored to personality type
- Detailed personality profiles including:
  - Core values and motivations
  - How they appear to others
  - Suitable career paths
  - Recommended academic majors

### 2. 1-on-1 Career Counseling
- **Personal career advisors** for individualized guidance
- Help understanding personality test results
- Career goal setting and academic planning
- Multiple counseling packages available
- Book sessions based on advisor availability
- Session management and booking history

### 3. Trial Programs
Two types of hands-on career exploration:

**VR Career Experiences**
- Virtual reality-powered career simulations
- Immersive workplace environment exploration
- Experience professions before committing
- AI-enhanced interactive scenarios

**Job Trials**
- Real-world exposure through partnerships with private sector
- Hands-on work experience in various industries
- Direct interaction with industry professionals
- Practical understanding of career requirements

### 4. College Exploration Hub
- **Comprehensive university database** across the Arab world
- Search and filter by:
  - Country and location
  - Academic programs and majors
  - Tuition fees and costs
  - Admission requirements
- Program details and prerequisites
- University rankings and information

### 5. Personalized Major Recommendations
- AI-powered major suggestions based on:
  - MBTI personality type
  - Personal interests and values
  - Career goals and aspirations
- Unlock additional recommendations through counseling sessions
- Detailed information about each suggested major

### 6. Multi-language Support
- **Arabic and English** interface
- Seamless language switching
- Culturally appropriate content for the Arab world
- Right-to-left (RTL) support for Arabic

## ğŸ“ Problem We Solve

### The Challenge
Over **51% of students** feel unsure about choosing their academic major, leading to:
- Poor academic performance due to lack of interest
- Wasted time and resources on unsuitable programs
- Career dissatisfaction and frequent job changes
- Unfulfilled potential and missed opportunities

### Our Solution
Siraj provides:
1. **Scientific personality assessment** to understand natural strengths
2. **Expert guidance** from qualified career counselors
3. **Practical experience** through trial programs
4. **Comprehensive information** about all available options
5. **Personalized recommendations** based on individual profiles
